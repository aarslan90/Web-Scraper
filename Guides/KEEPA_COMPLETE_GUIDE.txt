PART 3: UNDERSTANDING KEEPA GRAPHS (10 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Keepa shows you 3 main things:

1. ORANGE LINE = Amazon Price History
2. GREEN LINE = Sales Rank (BSR) History  
3. BLUE LINE = Buy Box Price History


Let me explain each one:


ORANGE LINE - AMAZON PRICE
---------------------------

What it shows:
   Price of the product over time

How to read it:

   HORIZONTAL LINE (Flat):
      ────────────────────
      Price is STABLE
      ✅ GOOD! Predictable profit
      Example: £15 → £15 → £15 (stable)

   RISING LINE (Going Up):
      ────────────────
             /
            /
           /
      Price INCREASING
      ✅ GOOD! Demand growing
      Example: £12 → £14 → £16 (rising)

   FALLING LINE (Going Down):
           \
            \
             \
      ────────────────
      Price CRASHING
      ❌ BAD! Product dying
      Example: £20 → £15 → £10 (crashing)
      DON'T BUY!

   VOLATILE (Up and Down):
         /\    /\
        /  \  /  \
       /    \/    \
      Unstable market
      ⚠️ RISKY! Unpredictable
      Example: £15 → £25 → £12 → £18


GREEN LINE - SALES RANK (BSR)
------------------------------

What it shows:
   How well product is selling
   Lower rank = More sales
   Rank drops when product sells!

IMPORTANT: Lower is BETTER!
   Rank 1,000 = Selling LOTS
   Rank 100,000 = Selling SLOW

How to read it:

   FREQUENT DIPS (Drops Down Often):
      ▁▁▁▁▁    ▁▁▁    ▁▁▁    ▁▁▁
      Product selling REGULARLY
      ✅ EXCELLENT! Sells multiple times per week
      Example: Rank drops to 5,000 many times

   SOME DIPS (Drops Sometimes):
      ▁▁▁▁▁         ▁▁▁         ▁▁▁
      Product selling OCCASIONALLY  
      ✅ GOOD! Sells few times per month
      Example: Rank drops to 20,000 occasionally

   FLAT HIGH (Stays High):
      ────────────────────────────
      Product NOT selling
      ❌ BAD! Nobody buying
      Example: Rank stays at 200,000
      DON'T BUY!

   RISING (Getting Worse):
                          ─────────
                      ───
                  ───
      Sales DECLINING
      ❌ BAD! Product dying
      Example: Rank was 30,000, now 150,000


BLUE LINE - BUY BOX PRICE
--------------------------

What it shows:
   Price of "Buy Box" (main buy button)
   Usually same as Amazon price

Look for:
   ✅ Matches orange line = Stable
   ⚠️ Different from orange = Multiple sellers changing price

PART 5: USING KEEPA IN YOUR WORKFLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FOR EACH PRODUCT YOU'RE RESEARCHING:


STEP 1: Check SellerAmp First
------------------------------

1. SellerAmp shows instant profitability

2. If profitable, continue to Keepa

3. If not profitable, skip (no need for Keepa)


STEP 2: Scroll to Keepa Graphs
-------------------------------

1. On the Amazon product page

2. Scroll down past product description

3. Find Keepa graphs (big chart)


STEP 3: Select Time Range
--------------------------

Above the graph, click time range:

• [1 month] - Too short
• [3 months] - ✅ GOOD for most products
• [6 months] - ✅ GOOD for seasonal check
• [1 year] - Best for trends
• [All] - See entire history

Recommendation: Use "3 months" first


STEP 4: Analyze Price (Orange Line)
------------------------------------

Ask yourself:

Q: Is price stable?
   ✅ Yes → Good!
   ❌ No, falling → SKIP

Q: Has price dropped >20% recently?
   ✅ No → Good!
   ❌ Yes → SKIP (dying product)

Q: Is price rising?
   ✅ Yes → Good! (demand growing)


STEP 5: Analyze Sales Rank (Green Line)
----------------------------------------

Ask yourself:

Q: Does rank dip frequently?
   ✅ Yes → Good! (selling regularly)
   ❌ No → Bad (not selling)

Q: How low do the dips go?
   ✅ Under 20,000 → EXCELLENT
   ✅ 20,000-50,000 → GOOD
   ⚠️ 50,000-100,000 → MODERATE
   ❌ Over 100,000 → SKIP

Q: Is rank getting better (lower) or worse (higher)?
   ✅ Getting better → Great! (sales increasing)
   ❌ Getting worse → Bad (sales declining)


STEP 6: Make Decision
----------------------

Combine SellerAmp + Keepa:

✅ BUY IF:
   • SellerAmp shows profit ✅
   • No IP alerts ✅
   • Keepa price stable ✅
   • Keepa rank dips frequently ✅

❌ SKIP IF:
   • SellerAmp shows loss ❌
   • OR IP alert ❌
   • OR Keepa price crashing ❌
   • OR Keepa rank always high ❌


STEP 7: Record in Excel
------------------------

In your Excel, add notes:

Example:
   "Keepa: Stable £15, BSR dips to 8K, GOOD"
   
   or
   
   "Keepa: Price falling, BSR rising, SKIP"

PART 8: KEEPA RED FLAGS - WHEN TO SKIP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🚩 RED FLAG 1: Price Dropped >20% in Last 30 Days
-------------------------------------------------

Example:
   Was: £20
   Now: £15
   Drop: 25%

What it means:
   • Product dying
   • Competition undercutting
   • Price war starting

ACTION: SKIP


🚩 RED FLAG 2: Rank Rising (Getting Worse)
------------------------------------------

Example:
   90 days ago: Rank 20,000
   60 days ago: Rank 35,000
   30 days ago: Rank 60,000
   Now: Rank 85,000

What it means:
   • Sales declining
   • Product losing popularity
   • Dying product

ACTION: SKIP


🚩 RED FLAG 3: No Rank Dips in 90 Days
--------------------------------------

Example:
   ────────────────────────
   Flat line at 120,000 for 3 months
   No dips at all

What it means:
   • Product not selling
   • Zero demand
   • Will sit in warehouse

ACTION: SKIP


🚩 RED FLAG 4: Massive Price Spikes
-----------------------------------

Example:
      /\
     /  \    /\
    /    \  /  \
   /      \/    \

What it means:
   • Unstable market
   • Can't predict profit
   • High risk

ACTION: SKIP


🚩 RED FLAG 5: Amazon Just Started Selling
------------------------------------------

Example:
   You see orange line + NEW yellow line appears
   Yellow line = Amazon as seller

What it means:
   • Amazon entered market
   • Will dominate Buy Box
   • Hard to compete

ACTION: SKIP or very cautious
