PART 2: STEP-BY-STEP PRODUCT HUNTING PROCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

You have Excel file with 2,000-5,000 warehouse products.
Now let's find the winners!


STEP 1: INITIAL FILTERING (EXCEL) - 30 minutes
───────────────────────────────────────────────

Open your scraped Excel file: London_Wholesale_Complete.xlsx

FILTER 1: Remove products with missing data
   • Delete rows with no Pack Price
   • Delete rows with no Pack Qty
   • Delete rows with no Product Name
   
   RESULT: ~1,500-4,000 products remaining


FILTER 2: Calculate estimated ROI (add Excel column)
   Formula: =(Amazon_Estimate - Warehouse_Cost - Fees) / Warehouse_Cost
   
   Where:
   • Amazon_Estimate = Pack_Price * 2.5 (rough estimate)
   • Warehouse_Cost = Pack_Price (from Excel)
   • Fees = Pack_Price * 0.35 (rough estimate: 15% referral + 20% FBA)
   
   QUICK FORMULA:
   =((B2*2.5) - B2 - (B2*0.35)) / B2
   
   FILTER: Keep only rows with estimated ROI > 40%
   
   RESULT: ~500-1,500 products remaining


FILTER 3: Price range filter
   • Remove products with Pack_Price < £3 (too cheap, low margin)
   • Remove products with Pack_Price > £50 (expensive, risky)
   
   SWEET SPOT: £5-£30 pack price
   
   RESULT: ~300-800 products remaining


FILTER 4: Category filter
   • Remove categories you can't sell without approval:
     ❌ Baby Care (often gated)
     ❌ Beauty (often gated)
     ❌ Groceries (gated)
     ❌ Medical (gated)
     ❌ Perfumes (restricted)
   
   • FOCUS ON:
     ✅ Household
     ✅ Kitchen & Cookware
     ✅ Pet Care
     ✅ Toys (if not gated)
     ✅ Tools & DIY
     ✅ Stationery
     ✅ Electrical Accessories
   
   RESULT: ~200-500 products remaining


STEP 2: AMAZON MATCHING - 2-3 hours
────────────────────────────────────

For each product (or at least top 200):

1. SEARCH ON AMAZON.CO.UK
   • Use product name from your Excel
   • Look for exact or similar match
   • Note the ASIN (product ID)

2. CHECK IF PRODUCT EXISTS
   ✅ Exact match found → Continue
   ⚠️ Similar product found → Might work (verify carefully)
   ❌ No match found → SKIP (don't create new listing)

3. RECORD AMAZON DATA (add columns to Excel)
   • ASIN
   • Current Amazon Price (Buy Box price)
   • BSR (Best Seller Rank)
   • Number of Sellers
   • Number of Reviews
   • Rating

4. QUICK CHECKS
   ❌ Amazon itself is selling? → SKIP (hard to compete)
   ❌ Price is £5 but you pay £8? → SKIP (unprofitable)
   ❌ 50+ sellers? → SKIP (too competitive)
   ❌ 10,000+ reviews? → SKIP (saturated)

RESULT: ~100-200 matched products

PART 7: COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

❌ MISTAKE 1: Buying without checking IP alerts
   COST: Account suspension, inventory seized
   FIX: Always use SellerAmp or check Brand Registry

❌ MISTAKE 2: Ignoring competition
   COST: Can't win Buy Box, no sales
   FIX: Skip products with 50+ sellers

❌ MISTAKE 3: Not checking Keepa
   COST: Buy product, price crashes next week
   FIX: Always check 90-day price history

❌ MISTAKE 4: Buying slow-moving products
   COST: Inventory sits for months, storage fees
   FIX: Only buy BSR < 100,000

❌ MISTAKE 5: Calculating profit wrong
   COST: Think it's profitable, actually losing money
   FIX: Include ALL fees (referral 15% + FBA £3-6 + shipping)

❌ MISTAKE 6: Buying oversized/heavy items
   COST: FBA fees eat all profit
   FIX: Stick to standard size, <2kg

❌ MISTAKE 7: Not verifying product exists on Amazon
   COST: Can't create listing, stuck with inventory
   FIX: ALWAYS find the ASIN before buying

❌ MISTAKE 8: Buying seasonal products off-season
   COST: Dead stock for 6-9 months
   FIX: Check Google Trends for year-round demand
